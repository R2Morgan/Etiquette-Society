The Etiquette Society

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Overview

The Etiquette Society ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Asociatia Domnilor Distinsi, a non-profit organization based in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use The Etiquette Society mobile application ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register and use the App:

  • Name — your first and last name, used to identify you within the member community
  • Email address — used for account authentication and password recovery
  • Phone number — optionally provided during registration
  • Profile picture — photos you choose to upload as your profile image
  • Username — your chosen member ID within the App
  • Device token — collected to send you push notifications about events and announcements

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the App experience:

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To display your profile within the member community
  • To send push notifications about upcoming events, posts, and announcements
  • To send password reset emails when requested
  • To manage your membership tier and associated benefits

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Stripe — membership payments are processed externally via Stripe through your web browser. We do not store or handle your payment information within the App.
  • Cloudflare R2 — profile pictures are stored securely using Cloudflare's storage infrastructure.
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — your device token is transmitted to Apple's servers solely to deliver push notifications.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email address below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Access to member data is restricted to authorized administrators only.

Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

The Etiquette Society is intended for adults only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify members of any significant changes via the App or by email.
